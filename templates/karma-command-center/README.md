# Karma Command Center

Karma Command Center is the operational planning, documentation, roadmap, architecture, and governance hub for Karma Infinity LLC.

This repository is intended to coordinate the work behind Karma Infinity’s mission-driven technology ecosystem before product code, public pilots, grants, partnerships, or deployment decisions are made.

## Legal status

Karma Infinity is operated as **Karma Infinity LLC**, a New Jersey limited liability company.

Karma Infinity LLC is not a nonprofit organization, charity, tax-exempt organization, public charity, foundation, or 501(c)(3). This repository must not imply nonprofit status, tax-exempt status, or tax-deductible charitable donation eligibility unless a future legal structure is formally created, reviewed, approved, and documented.

Use LLC-safe language such as:

- Funding
- Sponsorship
- Support
- Partnership
- Customer payment
- Revenue
- Eligible grant funding

Avoid language such as:

- Donation
- Charitable donation
- Tax-deductible donation
- Nonprofit donation
- 501(c)(3)
- Public charity
- Foundation

## Purpose

This repo exists to keep Karma Infinity organized, accountable, and legally accurate while the ecosystem grows.

- Security and privacy checkpoints

## High-risk review triggers

Escalate before publishing, merging, or acting on work involving:

- Health or mental health guidance
- Legal or financial guidance
- Crisis support
- Children, schools, clinics, governments, or vulnerable populations
- User data or sensitive personal information
- Payments, grants, sponsorships, investment, or revenue share
- Public claims about impact, outcomes, research, safety, or compliance
- AI agents that can act externally
- Security-sensitive infrastructure
