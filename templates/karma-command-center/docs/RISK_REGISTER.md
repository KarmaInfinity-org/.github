# Risk Register

## Purpose

Track operational, legal, security, privacy, accessibility, funding, and mission risks for Karma Infinity LLC.

## Risk scale

| Level | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Low | Manageable with standard review |
| Medium | Needs owner and mitigation |
| High | Requires review before launch or public use |
| Critical | Stop and escalate immediately |

## Risk template

```markdown
## Risk title

- Level: Low | Medium | High | Critical
- Owner:
- Status: Open | Mitigating | Closed
- Area: Legal | Security | Privacy | Accessibility | Funding | Product | AI | Operations | Reputation

### Description

What could go wrong?

### Impact

Who or what could be affected?

### Mitigation

What reduces the risk?

### Review trigger

What must happen before this moves forward?
```

## Active risks

## Legal-status confusion

- Level: High
- Owner: Maintainers
- Status: Open
- Area: Legal

### Description

Public content could accidentally describe Karma Infinity LLC as a nonprofit, charity, tax-exempt entity, or 501(c)(3).

### Impact

Could mislead contributors, funders, partners, or the public.

### Mitigation

Use LLC-safe language and review `LEGAL.md` before publishing public-facing language.

### Review trigger

Any funding, sponsorship, grant, partnership, public-benefit, or impact language.
